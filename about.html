Hi, I'm Gonzalo

I'm a Chilean designer currently living in Dublin, Ireland, and working at the Equifax's Dublin Development Center. I work designing and building the GCS Design System that supports myEquifax (Member Centre) solution in 3 different domains like US, UK and Canada.

I’m a passionate professional about the end-to-end design process and the collaboration with different areas that can drive success across an organization. My major goals have been to provide common tools, language and processes to connect technology and business through design.
